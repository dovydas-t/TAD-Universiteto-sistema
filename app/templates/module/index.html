About Modules

Your guide to understanding the study modules available

What are Study Modules?

Study modules are distinct units or subjects that make up your academic program. Each module focuses on a particular topic or skill and contributes to your overall qualification.

Why Modules Matter

Modules allow you to:

  • Specialize in areas of interest within your study program
  • Gain focused knowledge and skills in manageable segments
  • Track your academic progress clearly and efficiently
  • Choose elective modules that align with your career goals

Types of Modules

  • Core Modules: Mandatory courses you must complete
  • Elective Modules: Optional courses to broaden your knowledge
  • Practical Modules: Hands-on training and labs

How to Choose the Right Modules

Consider your interests, career plans, and the requirements of your study program. Consult with your academic advisor to create a balanced module plan.

For more detailed information about specific modules, please visit your study programs page.
