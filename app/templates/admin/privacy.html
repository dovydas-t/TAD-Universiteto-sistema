Privacy Policy

The concepts used in the Privacy Policy are interpreted in the meaning defined as follows:

  • Personal data – any information related to a natural person whose identity is or can be identified...
  • GDPR – Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and Council...
  • Data subject, Data controller, Processor, Joint controllers…
  • Special category personal data – racial or ethnic origin, health, etc.
  • Consent – clear agreement to data processing.
  • Direct marketing – promotion requiring consent.

We apply appropriate organizational and technical measures, and employees follow GDPR and national regulations.

Data is processed lawfully, fairly, securely, and accurately. We may transfer data outside the EU/EEA with protection mechanisms like Standard Contractual Clauses.

  • Right to know about data processing
  • Right to access, correct, delete, limit, or transfer data
  • Right to object or withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the State Data Protection Inspectorate

We ensure security through technical safeguards and authorized processors. Transfers outside the EU/EEA are handled using secure contracts or adequacy decisions.

This Privacy Policy may be updated based on legal or procedural changes. The latest version is always available at ktu.edu.
